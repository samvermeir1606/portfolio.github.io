About me

UX designer, gameplay programmer.

I'm Sam Vermeir

A passionate 27-year-old UX designer, from Dendermonde (Belgium), currently working at VRT, the Flemish public broadcaster. With a background in game design from Howest (Hogeschool West Vlaanderen) and my current role as Product Designer at VRT, I bring a unique blend of expertise in interactive and UX design to my projects.

In my free time, I love exploring new horizons and pushing the boundaries of my skillset. Whether it's delving into the realms of VR/AR, building captivating websites, developing games, creating stunning 3D renders, or crafting AI for games, I am constantly driven by a desire to learn and innovate.
